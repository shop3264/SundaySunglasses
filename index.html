Our Story

It all started under the cloud-ridden April sky of yet another belated Swedish spring in 2008. 

Two friends had a vision: to spread happiness by turning an everyday essential into a colorful design piece with a rigid standard of ultimate quality, craftsmanship and creativity. A concept now brought to perfection by the Happy Socks collective of creators. Today Happy Socks are sold in more than 90 countries and on every continent.
Simply put: there's a pair of high-quality socks and underwear for every occasion, mindset and style.

With the HAPPY SOCKS brand motto being “All play. No work” it was only natural for international cult eyewear brand SUNDAY SOMEWHERE to team up and create a limited edition range of sunglasses.
